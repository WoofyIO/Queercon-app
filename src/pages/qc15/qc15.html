Queercon 15 @ Defcon Wed Aug 8 - Sun Aug 12 The main event is back for our fifteenth year! Everything you need to know will be here as the information comes avalible. In the mean time we have made an important anouncement as to how we will be running events this year. Check out the full anouncement bellow, but here is the TL;DR:
  • Queercon will have two different types of Badges this year: Our usual (and fantastic) Electronic Badge, as well as a Non-Electronic Badge.
  • A Queercon Badge (Electronic or Non) will be required for attendance to many of our evening events.
  • In continuing tradition, a portion of the limited-edition Queercon Electronic Badges will be available for reservation by a one-time donation in the upcoming weeks.
  • We are also making Non-Electronic Badges available by donation this year.
  • There will NOT be any method to reserve, or donate for, Electronic Badges onsite in Las Vegas. However, Non-Electronic Badges will be available for donation onsite.
Where Is It? Queercon 15 is part of Defcon 26 at Caesar's Palace in Las Vegas. Nearly all of our events are within Caesar's (and the rare exceptions usually have a shuttle from Caesar's). Queercon Room Block (SOLD OUT) Sadly we were not able to secure the same size of room block as we're all used to, and all rooms are taken. Schedule of Events We will have social gatherings, parties (including our legendary Friday Night Pool Party), and new for this year our own closing ceremony. Most of the events are in our private hotel chillout lounge. The full schedule of events will be posted soon; check here for the earlist release of schedule! Queercon Badge Every year, we create our own custom electronic Queercon badge (electronic badge is not required to attend events). Some badges are sold in advance to help pay for the event, but every year many of them are given away for free to attendees so everyone gets a chance to join the fun.
